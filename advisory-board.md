# Advisory Board

Use this file to track reviewer-style roles for later review-board runs.

## Suggested roles

- Experimental syntax methodologist.
- Psychometrician or measurement theorist.
- Corpus and benchmark-data specialist.
- Ethics/research-design reviewer for no-new-data claims.
- Theoretical syntactician concerned with grammaticality vs acceptability.

## Standing review questions

- Does the paper overclaim replication when it has only secondary data?
- Are task effects, response scales, and theoretical labels kept separate?
- Does the paper fairly preserve Bard et al.'s contribution before revising it?
- Does the contemporary benchmark material change the construct rather than merely extend it?
- Are source and dataset claims verified before citation?
